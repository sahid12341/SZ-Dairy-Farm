Return Requirements

At SZ Dairy Farm, we take the quality and safety of our products seriously. Due to the perishable nature of dairy products, we have specific guidelines for returns:

  • Products must be returned within 24 hours of purchase
  • Original receipt must be presented
  • Products must in their original packaging
  • Temperature-sensitive items must be properly stored before return

Return Process

Step 1: Inspection

Our team will inspect the returned product for quality and storage conditions.

Step 2: Verification

We'll verify your purchase with the original receipt.

Step 3: Processing

Once approved, we'll process your refund immediately.

Important Note

For food safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened dairy products
  • Products without original packaging
  • Products showing signs of improper storage
  • Products past their expiration date

Quality Guarantee

If you receive a damaged or spoiled product, we'll replace it or provide a full refund after inspection, no questions asked!

Refund Process

Refunds will be processed in the original form of payment:

  • Credit card refunds: Immediate
  • Debit card refunds: Immediate
  • Cash refunds: Immediate
